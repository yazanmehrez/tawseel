Your fast way for MOHRE services

Tawseel is a mobile service offers the services of Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (MOHRE) to its customers on their premises at their convenient time.

The customer can request Tawseel service either from Tawseel mobile application or by calling Tawseel call center. He can choose the services he would like to get, the suitable time and location for him that he will be at. Then, Tawseel vehicle heads to be at the chosen location on time to fulfill his needs.
